EMA Trend Crossover (strat_01_ema_cross.py):

Asset focus: BTC/USD

Timeframe: 1 Hour bars.

Logic: Calculate EMA_12 (Fast) and EMA_26 (Slow).

Entry: Go LONG (buy) when EMA_12 crosses above EMA_26.

Exit/Risk: Sell to close when EMA_12 crosses below EMA_26.

Constraint: Never short. Alpaca crypto currently only supports Spot (Long) trading.

Bollinger Volatility Breakout (strat_02_bb_breakout.py):

Asset focus: ETH/USD

Timeframe: 15 Minute bars.

Logic: Calculate Bollinger Bands (Length=20, StdDev=2) and Bandwidth ((Upper - Lower) / SMA_20). Track 15m Volume SMA (Length=20).

Entry: Squeeze condition: Bandwidth falls to the lowest level in the last 50 periods. Breakout condition: Current close > Upper Band AND Current Volume > Volume SMA * 1.5. Execute MARKET BUY.

Exit: Trailing stop loss of 3% or exit when close drops below the SMA_20 middle band.

Dynamic Spot Grid (strat_03_grid_spot.py):

Asset focus: SOL/USD

Logic: Market Maker strategy. Establish a baseline price based on the 24H VWAP.

Execution: Divide allocated capital into 10 tranches. Place 5 resting LIMIT BUY orders at intervals of -1.5%, -3.0%, -4.5%, -6.0%, -7.5% below the VWAP. As soon as a BUY is filled, immediately place a LIMIT SELL order for that exact tranche amount at +1.5% above the filled price.

State Tracking: This strategy requires keeping a local SQLite or dictionary state of active limit order_ids to cancel and replace them if the VWAP baseline drifts more than 5%.
